# DateTruncUnit

Date truncation units for time-based grouping.  Used to group data by time periods.  Values:     YEAR: Group by year     MONTH: Group by month     WEEK: Group by week     DAY: Group by day     HOUR: Group by hour     MINUTE: Group by minute     SECOND: Group by second  Examples:     - Use DAY to group video uploads by day     - Use MONTH to analyze monthly trends

## Enum

* `YEAR` (value: `'year'`)

* `MONTH` (value: `'month'`)

* `WEEK` (value: `'week'`)

* `DAY` (value: `'day'`)

* `HOUR` (value: `'hour'`)

* `MINUTE` (value: `'minute'`)

* `SECOND` (value: `'second'`)
